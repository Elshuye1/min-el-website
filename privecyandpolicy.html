1. Introduction

Welcome to the Min-El Platform. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your business information with the highest standards of security. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data across our entire platform, including the Dashboard, Learning Hub, and Community features.

2. Data We Collect

2.1. Information You Provide

This includes data you submit when registering or using our services:

  • **Business Identity:** Business Name, Registration Details, Industry Type.
  • **Personal Identity:** Full Name, Email Address, Password (encrypted), and Primary Language.
  • **Financial Data:** Sales Records, Expense Reports, Inventory Levels, and Cost Prices (for business management).

2.2. Data Collected Automatically

When you use the Community or Dashboard, we automatically record:

  • **Usage Data:** Posts, Videos, Photos, Comments, Likes, Shares, Views, and activity logs.
  • **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • **Location Data:** General location based on your IP address for security and regional content purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to operate and improve the Min-El service:

  • To provide the Core Platform functionality (Dashboard, Inventory, Learning Hub).
  • To display your content and engage with the Community (posts, videos, etc.).
  • To ensure security, detect fraud, and prevent unauthorized access using Row-Level Security (RLS).
  • For internal research and statistical analysis to improve our algorithms and content offerings.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your business data is **never** shared externally. We may share limited personal data only when necessary:

  • **With Service Providers:** Third parties who provide essential services (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • **Legal Compliance:** When legally required by government authorities or court orders.
  • **Community Content:** Your username and public posts/videos are visible to other community members.

5. Security Measures

Min-El employs rigorous security protocols, including:

  • Encryption of data both in transit and at rest.
  • Implementation of Row-Level Security (RLS) to isolate and protect each user's financial data.
  • Regular security audits and compliance checks.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or limit the use of your personal data. You can exercise these rights through your profile settings or by contacting us.

7. Community & Ad Revenue Sharing

Min-El runs advertisements from partners (including Google AdSense) on the Community page. These policies govern the advertising structure:

  • **Content Ownership:** You retain all rights to the videos, photos, and letters you post.
  • **Ad Placement:** By posting public content, you grant Min-El a worldwide, royalty-free license to display advertisements adjacent to or within your content.
  • **Monetization:** Any revenue generated from general ad spaces is solely for Min-El. Specific programs for revenue sharing with content creators will be announced separately.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by email or through a prominent notice on our platform before the changes take effect.
